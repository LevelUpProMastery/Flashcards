Week 1 – Business Basics

Core foundations for GCSE Business

Topics

  • Business aims and objectives
  • Stakeholders
  • Business ownership

Learn

  • Stakeholder – anyone with an interest in a business, such as customers, employees and owners
  • Revenue – the total income a business receives from sales
  • Costs – the money a business spends to operate
  • Profit – revenue minus costs
  • Business objective – a goal a business wants to achieve, such as profit, growth or survival
  • Sole trader – a business owned by one person
  • Partnership – a business owned by between 2 and 20 people
  • Limited company – a business owned by shareholders with limited liability

Apply

1. State one stakeholder of a business. (2)

Answer: One stakeholder is employees.

2. Identify one business objective. (2)

Answer: One business objective is to make a profit.

3. Explain one reason why profit is important. (4)

Model: Profit is important because it allows the business to survive. This means the business can continue operating and pay its costs. This leads to long-term stability.

Develop

Question: Explain one reason why stakeholders are important to a business. (6)

Model Answer: One reason stakeholders are important is because they can influence business decisions. For example, customers may demand better quality products or lower prices. This means the business may need to adapt what it offers. This leads to improved customer satisfaction and possibly higher sales. Therefore the business may increase its revenue and profit.

Master

Question: Analyse the importance of business objectives to a business. (9)

  • Business objectives give direction to a business. Because they provide clear goals, this leads to better decision making and a stronger sense of purpose.
  • They also help measure success. Because a business can compare its performance against its objectives, this leads to improved planning and better performance management.
  • Conclusion: Business objectives are important because they guide decisions and help the business improve over time.

Exam Tip

Use BLT in all extended answers: Because → Leads to → Therefore. This helps you fully develop your points and access higher marks.
