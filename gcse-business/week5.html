Week 5 – Business Operations

Topics

  • Production processes
  • Efficiency and productivity
  • Quality
  • Customer service
  • Procurement and stock control

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Production – the process of making goods or delivering services
  • Productivity – output per worker over a period of time
  • Efficiency – producing output with minimum waste, cost or time
  • Quality – how well a product meets customer expectations
  • Customer service – support given to customers before, during and after a purchase

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one advantage of good customer service for a business (2)

2. Identify one way a business can improve productivity (2)

3. Explain one benefit of producing high-quality products (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one reason why efficiency is important to a business.

Sentence starter: One reason efficiency is important is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse the importance of quality for a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because high quality can improve customer satisfaction...
  • This leads to repeat purchases and a better reputation...
  • Therefore sales and profit may increase...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: High quality → satisfied customers → repeat business → increased sales
  • Point 2: Better quality → fewer returns and complaints → lower costs
  • Conclusion: Quality is important because it can improve both revenue and reputation

Exam Tip

In operations questions, link your answer to costs, efficiency, customer satisfaction and profit.
