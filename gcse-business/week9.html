Topics

  • Mixed topics from across the course
  • Applying knowledge to case studies
  • Connecting business functions
  • Improving exam technique

Learn

  • Revise key definitions from all units
  • Review your weakest topics from earlier weeks
  • Go over key formulas such as profit and break-even
  • Practise linking one topic to another in answers
  • Focus on how marketing, finance, HR and operations connect

Apply

1. State one factor that could influence demand for a product. (2)

2. Identify one method a business could use to motivate employees. (2)

3. Explain one impact of increased competition on a business. (4)

Develop

Explain one impact of a business improving its marketing. (6)

Sentence starter: One impact of improved marketing is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master

Analyse how different business functions work together to improve performance. (9)

Use BLT: Because marketing attracts customers... Leads to... Therefore...

Exam Tip

In synoptic questions, try to connect at least two areas of the course, for example marketing and operations, or finance and human resources.
