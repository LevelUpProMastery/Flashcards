Week 9 – Synoptic Practice

Focus

  • Mixed topics from across the course
  • Applying knowledge to case studies
  • Developing exam technique
  • Identifying weak areas

Learn

  • Revise key definitions from ALL topics
  • Focus on weak areas identified in mocks
  • Review key formulas (profit, break-even)

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one factor that influences demand for a product (2)

2. Identify one method of motivation a business could use (2)

3. Explain one impact of increased competition on a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one impact of a business improving its marketing.

Sentence starter: One impact of improved marketing is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse how different business functions work together to improve performance.

Use BLT:

  • Because marketing attracts customers...
  • This leads to increased demand and higher sales...
  • Therefore operations must increase output to meet demand...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Marketing → increased demand → operations increase production → higher sales
  • Point 2: HR → motivated staff → better productivity → improved efficiency
  • Conclusion: Business functions must work together to maximise performance

Exam Tip

In synoptic questions, link different topics together (e.g. marketing + operations + HR).
