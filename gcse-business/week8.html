Week 8 – Finance

Topics

  • Costs, revenue and profit
  • Fixed and variable costs
  • Break-even
  • Cash flow
  • Sources of finance

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Revenue – income from sales
  • Costs – money spent by a business
  • Profit – revenue minus costs
  • Fixed costs – costs that do not change with output
  • Variable costs – costs that change with output
  • Break-even – the point where total revenue equals total costs
  • Cash flow – money flowing in and out of a business

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one example of a fixed cost (2)

2. Identify one source of finance for a business (2)

3. Explain one benefit of breaking even for a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one reason why managing cash flow is important to a business.

Sentence starter: One reason cash flow is important is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse the importance of profit for a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because profit allows a business to survive and grow...
  • This leads to reinvestment and expansion...
  • Therefore the business becomes more successful long-term...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Profit → ability to reinvest → growth → increased market share
  • Point 2: Profit → financial security → able to survive difficult periods
  • Conclusion: Profit is essential for long-term survival and success

Exam Tip

In finance questions, always show calculations clearly and link answers to profit or survival.
