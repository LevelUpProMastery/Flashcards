Week 4 – External Environment

Topics

  • Economic climate
  • Interest rates
  • Legislation
  • Competition
  • Changes in demand

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Interest rates – cost of borrowing money
  • Inflation – increase in prices over time
  • Legislation – laws businesses must follow
  • Demand – willingness and ability to buy
  • Competition – rival businesses in the market

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one effect of rising interest rates on a business (2)

2. Identify one example of business legislation (2)

3. Explain one impact of inflation on a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one impact of government legislation on a business.

Sentence starter: One impact of legislation is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse the impact of economic changes on a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because economic changes affect customer spending...
  • This leads to higher/lower demand...
  • Therefore revenue and profit may change...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Inflation → higher costs → reduced profit margins
  • Point 2: Interest rates ↑ → less borrowing → less investment
  • Conclusion: Economic factors are crucial as they directly impact demand and costs

Exam Tip

Always show a clear chain of analysis: cause → effect → impact on profit.
