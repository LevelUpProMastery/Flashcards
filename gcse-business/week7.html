Week 7 – Marketing

Topics

  • Market research
  • Market segmentation
  • The marketing mix
  • Product, price, place and promotion
  • Target market

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Market research – collecting information about customers, competitors and the market
  • Primary research – research collected first-hand for a specific purpose
  • Secondary research – research collected by someone else and already available
  • Market segmentation – dividing a market into groups of customers with similar needs
  • Target market – the specific group of customers a business aims to sell to

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one method of primary market research (2)

2. Identify one benefit of market segmentation (2)

3. Explain one advantage of promotion for a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one reason why market research is important to a business.

Sentence starter: One reason market research is important is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse the importance of the marketing mix to a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because the right product and price can attract customers...
  • This leads to increased demand and stronger sales...
  • Therefore revenue and profit may rise...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Effective product and pricing decisions → better customer appeal → higher sales
  • Point 2: Strong promotion and place → greater awareness and availability → more customers
  • Conclusion: The marketing mix is important because it helps a business meet customer needs and compete effectively

Exam Tip

In marketing questions, always link your point to customers, demand, sales and profit.
