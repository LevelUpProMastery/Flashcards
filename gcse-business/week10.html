Week 10 – Final Exam Preparation

Focus

  • Full past papers
  • Timing and exam technique
  • Confidence building
  • Final knowledge checks

Learn

  • Revise key definitions across all topics
  • Review formulas (profit, revenue, break-even)
  • Go over common exam mistakes

Apply

  • Complete at least one full past paper
  • Mark using the mark scheme
  • Identify areas to improve

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one way a business can improve its performance.

Sentence starter: One way a business can improve performance is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse how a business can achieve success in a competitive market.

Use BLT:

  • Because businesses must attract customers...
  • This leads to increased sales and market share...
  • Therefore profit and long-term success may improve...

Exam Technique

  • Read the question carefully
  • Use BLT for 6 and 9 markers
  • Answer what the question is asking
  • Use full sentences and key terms
  • Manage your time carefully

Final Tip

You are ready. Stay calm, trust your revision, and focus on clear structured answers.
