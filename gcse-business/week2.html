Week 2 – Location & Growth

Topics

  • Business location
  • Footfall
  • Online vs physical location
  • Business expansion
  • Globalisation

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Location – where a business is based
  • Footfall – number of people passing a business
  • Online business – operates via the internet
  • Expansion – growth of a business
  • Globalisation – operating in more than one country

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one reason why location is important for a business (2)

2. Identify one benefit of a business operating online (2)

3. Explain one advantage of high footfall for a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one advantage of a business expanding.

Sentence starter: One advantage of expansion is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse the importance of location for a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because location affects customers...
  • This leads to higher/lower sales...
  • Therefore profits may increase/decrease...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Good location → more customers → more sales → more profit
  • Point 2: Poor location → low visibility → fewer customers → lower revenue
  • Conclusion: Location is very important, especially for physical businesses

Exam Tip

Always link back to profit, sales or customers to secure full marks.
