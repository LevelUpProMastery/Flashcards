Week 3 – Influences on Business

Topics

  • Technology
  • Ethics
  • Environmental considerations
  • Economic climate
  • Competition

Learn (Key Definitions)

  • Technology – use of machinery and systems in business
  • Ethics – doing what is morally right
  • Environmental impact – effect on the environment
  • Economic climate – how well the economy is performing
  • Competition – other businesses offering similar products

Apply (2–4 markers)

1. State one way technology can improve a business (2)

2. Identify one ethical issue a business may face (2)

3. Explain one impact of competition on a business (4)

Develop (6 marker)

Explain one impact of technology on a business.

Sentence starter: One impact of technology is... This means... This leads to... Therefore...

Master (9 marker)

Analyse how external factors influence a business.

Use BLT:

  • Because changes in the economy can affect demand...
  • This leads to higher/lower sales...
  • Therefore profits may increase/decrease...

Model Answer Structure

  • Point 1: Technology → increased efficiency → lower costs → higher profit
  • Point 2: Competition → price pressure → reduced profit margins
  • Conclusion: External factors are important as businesses must adapt to survive

Exam Tip

Always link external factors back to sales, costs or profit to access higher marks.
